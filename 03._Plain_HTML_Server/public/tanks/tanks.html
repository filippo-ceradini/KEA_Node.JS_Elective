Tanks

About Us

The Museum of Tanks is dedicated to preserving the history of tanks and their role in military conflicts around the world. Our collection includes tanks from different eras and countries, and we offer exhibitions and educational programs for all ages.

Exhibitions

Our exhibitions showcase the most important tanks in history, from World War I to the present day. We also offer interactive displays, videos, and guided tours to help visitors learn about the technology and strategy behind these machines.

Events

We host a variety of events throughout the year, including talks, workshops, and film screenings. Check our calendar for upcoming events.

Visit Us

We are located in the heart of the city, and our opening hours are from 10am to 6pm every day. Admission is free for children under 12, and tickets for adults are $10. We also offer discounts for groups and students.
